How To Find Perimeter And Area Of a 2-d Shapes

To Find Perimeter of 2-d shapes we have to add all sides

For Example :-

To Find the Perimeter of Rectangle Length=30 Breadth=40

30 + 30 + 40 + 40 OR 2(30) + 2(40)

To Find Area of 2-d shapes we have to Multiply side by side

For Example :-

To Find Area of Rectangle Length=20 Breadth=15

l*b = 20*15
